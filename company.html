Berkshire Hathaway Inc
Berkshire Hathaway Inc. is a massive American multinational conglomerate holding company headquartered in Omaha, Nebraska, which served as the investment vehicle for Warren Buffett for six decades before he stepped down as CEO at the end of 2025. While not a traditional mutual fund, it operates similarly by holding a diversified portfolio of companies and stocks, with a market capitalization exceeding $1 trillion as of 2024.
As of late 2025, Berkshire was sitting on a record cash hoard, estimated between $334 billion and $382 billion, which it uses to buy companies or invest in market dips.
